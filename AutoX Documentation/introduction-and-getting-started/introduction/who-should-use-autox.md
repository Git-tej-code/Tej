# Who Should Use AutoX?

**AutoX is designed for cross-functional teams, including:**

* Business Users\
  Define workflows, logic, and operational intent
* Power Users\
  Design and execute advanced workflows
* Agent Developers\
  Build, test, and version AI agents
* Integration Engineers\
  Configure system connectivity and execution logic
* Platform & IT Admins\
  Manage users, access, deployments, and governance
* Leadership & Compliance Teams\
  Monitor usage, cost, performance, and audit trails
