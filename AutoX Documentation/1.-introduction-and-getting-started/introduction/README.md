# Introduction

AutoX is an enterprise-grade agentic automation platform that enables organizations to orchestrate business processes, AI agents, integrations, and human decision points within a governed and observable environment. It is designed to help teams move beyond rigid, rule-based automation toward intelligent, scalable, and auditable workflows.

This section introduces the vision, purpose, and foundational concepts of AutoX, helping new users understand what the platform is, why it exists, and how it is structured before they begin using it.

**In this section, you will learn:**

* What AutoX is and what problems it solves
* The vision and mission behind the platform
* Who should use AutoX and this documentation
* Core concepts that underpin the platform
